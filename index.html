How We Work

Our Recruitment Flow

A rigorous, end-to-end process ensuring only the most qualified aviation professionals reach your team.

01
Needs Analysis

We thoroughly understand your specific requirements — role responsibilities, required skill sets, and organisational culture — before starting.

02
Candidate Sourcing

We tap into our extensive database of 5,000+ aviation professionals and use targeted search strategies across multiple regions to identify top talent.

03
Screening & Shortlisting

We carefully screen candidates to assess qualifications, experience, and alignment — ensuring only the best fit moves forward in the process.

04
Interviews & Assessments

We facilitate interviews between you and shortlisted candidates — enabling both parties to assess suitability and cultural fit within your organisation.

05
Skill Trade Tests

To validate practical abilities, we administer skill trade tests on-site or remotely — ensuring candidates meet the required technical standards for the role.

06
Ongoing HR Support & Onboarding

We provide continuous HR support throughout the process, ensuring smooth onboarding and fostering a positive, long-term relationship between all parties.
